2026.05.27 AI Application Operations

借力 AI 提效赋能，深耕服务聚力前行

Leveraging AI for Efficiency, Deepening Service for Growth

华盛召开 AI 工具应用及群组管理专项会议，从内容表达、报价准备到组织协同，把 AI 带入真实业务流程。

Huasheng held a special meeting on AI tool applications and group management, bringing AI into real business workflows from content expression and quotation preparation to organizational collaboration.

峰会速睇

华盛的工作日常，始终围绕一个完整流程展开：客户提出需求，团队理解项目，整理资料，形成报价，完善方案，呈现产品，再通过持续沟通推动项目落地。AI 进入企业，不是为了替代这些工作，而是为了最大化利用工具，把每个环节做得更快、更准、更清楚。

核心指导建议：让 AI 进入华盛真实业务

会议开场，梁明忠董事长点明方向：AI 的学习与运用，是传统企业继续发展的根和方向。对华盛来说，“根”是长期积累的制造能力、项目经验和客户服务；“方向”是把 AI 带进实际工作流程，让工具真正成为提效增速的能力。

聚焦国际前沿 AI 模型应用趋势

刘智恒通过新加坡远程视频参会，介绍国际前沿 AI 模型、相关软件和行业动态，为团队打开外部视野。看见趋势之后，更重要的是回到华盛自己的现场：哪些工作最耗时，哪些信息最容易断点，哪些环节可以先交给 AI 辅助。

AI 工具的运用：实用性能及实际使用

梁逸亨结合 PPT 和产品实拍案例，讲解 AI 的功能、模型、应用方法和局限。他提出的 6 个月 30% 提效目标，指向的是流程升级：资料整理、方案表达、图片处理、报价准备，都可以先由 AI 辅助，再由人判断、校准和负责。

AI 不会替代你，
但会用 AI 的人，会替代不会用 AI 的人。

认知驱动：如何把 AI 变成长期创意搭档

梁思彦提出，AI 可以帮助撰写文案、润色内容、处理图片，但更重要的是成为人的“第二视角”。AI 时代的内容工作需要审美系统化、判断逻辑化、内容风格化，也需要理解目标人群的情绪和需求。产品不只是被制造出来，还要被客户清楚理解、准确感知。

实操示范：AI 如何嵌入华盛工作

真正让现场产生讨论的，是袁娇丽经理分享的公交站牌项目报价案例。她展示 AI 如何辅助资料整理、报价思路、出图美工和客户对接，引发各部门厂长和管理层的追问与讨论。报价不是简单填数字，它背后是需求理解、方案组织、成本逻辑、图片表达和客户沟通。

会议后续落实：用 AI 优化流程

黄恒光从法务、文案、商务报价、技术方案编制等维度补充 AI 应用经验。朱腾飞围绕各类工作微信群，讲解群组梳理、分类管理和日常运维方案。重点不只是“建群”，而是让会议成果能够持续落实：沉淀案例、交流问题、跟进模板，推动各部门真正用起来。

用 AI 优化流程，用行动升级品牌能力

忠总要求全体人员将 AI 工具全面运用到报价、方案设计、客户服务等工作中。佳总也围绕 AI 学习与落地应用提出问题与建议，推动 AI 从少数人的兴趣，变成全员可用的工作能力。

对老客户而言，华盛推进 AI 不是制造新概念，而是为了让需求响应更快、报价逻辑更清楚、方案表达更专业、沟通协同更顺畅。

Meeting Snapshot

Huasheng's daily work follows a complete business flow: customers raise requirements, the team understands the project, organizes materials, prepares quotations, improves solutions, presents products, and drives execution through continuous communication. AI is not entering the company to replace these tasks. It is here to make each step faster, more accurate, and clearer.

Core Direction: Bring AI into Real Work

At the opening, Chairman Liang Mingzhong set the direction: learning and applying AI is both the root and direction for a traditional enterprise's continued development. For Huasheng, the root is accumulated manufacturing capability, project experience, and customer service. The direction is to bring AI into actual workflows.

Global Trends in Frontier AI Applications

Liu Zhiheng joined remotely from Singapore and introduced frontier AI models, software applications, and industry trends. The next task is to return to Huasheng's own operation: which tasks take time, where information breaks down, and which steps can first be supported by AI.

AI Tools: Practical Performance and Real Use

Liang Yiheng explained AI functions, models, application methods, and limitations through PPT materials and real product cases. His six-month 30% efficiency target points to workflow upgrading: material organization, solution presentation, image processing, and quotation preparation can first be supported by AI, then judged and corrected by people.

AI will not replace you.
But people who use AI will replace people who do not.

Cognition-Driven: Turning AI into a Creative Partner

Liang Siyan noted that AI can help write copy, polish content, and process images, but more importantly, it can become a second perspective for people. Content work in the AI era requires systematic aesthetics, logical judgment, consistent style, and an understanding of audience needs.

Practical Demo: AI in Huasheng Workflows

The case that triggered the most discussion was the bus shelter quotation project shared by Manager Yuan Jiaoli. She showed how AI supports material organization, quotation logic, visual output, and customer communication. A quotation is never just numbers. It includes requirement understanding, solution structure, cost logic, image expression, and customer communication.

Follow-Up Implementation: Optimizing Workflows with AI

Huang Hengguang supplemented AI application experience across legal work, copywriting, commercial quotation plans, and technical proposal preparation. Zhu Tengfei explained the sorting, classification, and daily operation of work WeChat groups. The focus is not simply creating groups, but making meeting outcomes continuously actionable.

Upgrade Brand Capability through Action

General Manager Zhong asked all staff to apply AI tools across quotation, solution design, and customer service. General Manager Jia also raised questions and suggestions on AI learning and implementation, helping move AI from an interest of a few people to a capability available to the whole team.

For long-term customers, Huasheng's AI initiative is not about creating a new concept. It is about faster response, clearer quotation logic, more professional solution presentation, and smoother communication and collaboration.
